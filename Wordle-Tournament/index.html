How to Play
Welcome to WORDLE Tournament! Play three Levels and Climb up the Leaderboard
To start ENTER your name and click on 'Start Game'.
NOTE:For each Game involving N letters you get N+1 tries.
You will have to pass three levels of Wordle with word lengths 5, 6 and 7.

COLOR SPECIFIER::
Green:The character entered is at the correct position as the answer.
Yellow: The character entered is present in the answer but not at the given position.
Grey: The character entered is not present in the answer.
