How to Play
Welcome to AlphaNumericle! You have to guess a word/number of the length chosen by you.
Drag the slider to adjust the length of the word/number.
NOTE:For each Game involving N characters you get N+1 tries.
Wordle only accepts valid dictionary words

COLOR SPECIFIER::
Green:The character entered is at the correct position as the answer.
Yellow: The character entered is present in the answer but not at the given position.
Grey: The character entered is not present in the answer.
